Define the data model and terminology for a recipe sharing application called CookBook.
Users can create recipes with ingredients, steps, photos, and nutritional info.
Recipes can be tagged with categories like cuisine type, dietary restrictions, and difficulty level.
Users can follow other users, save recipes to collections, and leave ratings with reviews.
The app also needs meal planning where users can assign recipes to calendar dates.
Establish consistent naming conventions for all entities and their relationships.
